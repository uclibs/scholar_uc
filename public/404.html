Temporary Content Freeze!

In preparation for a major upgrade to Scholar@UC, we have temporarily put all content in view-only mode in order to ensure your data stays consistent through the upgrade.
While Scholar@UC is in view-only mode, users will not be able to:

  • Create or edit works
  • Create or edit collections
  • Create or edit groups
  • Edit their profile page
Users that have never logged into Scholar@UC in the past will not be able to log in while the site is in view-only mode.

If you feel this does not apply to the page you are trying to access, please try the tips below.

Page Not Found

The page you are looking for may have been removed, had its name changed, or is temporarily unavailable.

Please try the following:

  • If you typed the page address in the Address bar, make sure that it is spelled correctly.
  • Go to scholar.uc.edu, and then look for links to the information you want.
  • Click the Back button on your browser to try another link.
  • If you reached this page using a bookmark, the page you're looking for may have moved.
  • Contact us for assistance.
